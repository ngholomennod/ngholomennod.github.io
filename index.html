about me

my name is esmé, and i'm an undergraduate computer science student in the southeast united states.


aside from my school and professional life, my main hobbies all center around the idea of tinkering -- carving little things out of wood, repurposing old technology, or prototyping something entirely new out of whatever i can get my hands on. sometimes, these projects are for pragmatic reasons like wanting a feeding timer displayed on my ball python's tank to remind me when it's time to get him another mouse; other times, i make things as gifts or just for the sake of putting together a piece of art that i can be proud of.
